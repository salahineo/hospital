Visa to Travel to Jordan

Most nationalities do not need a visa to enter Jordan. A Visa can be obtained upon arrival to Queen Alia International Airport (QAIA) or any other land borders to Jordan with a fee of 40 JOD paid upon issuing.

Some nationalities require special pre-approval before being granted a visa to enter Jordan. Please check with our International Patients team to double check visa and entry requirements whereby they will handle your case and facilitate the procedures

TO BE WELL-PREPARED FOR YOUR TRIP, PLEASE VISIT THE FOLLOWING LINK AHEAD OF YOUR TRAVE TIME FOR MORE INFORMATION ABOUT COVID-19 REGULATIONS WHEN TRAVELING TO JORDAN

https://www.visitjordan.gov.jo/

Abdali Hospital provides Meet & Greet services at Queen Alia International Airport (QAIA) upon request.

Our meet and greet services include: 

 

  • Meet and Greet upon arrival at Queen Alia International Airport (QAIA).
  • Porter Service and assistance at the check-in counter.
  • Fast-track through check-in, Immigration, and Security.
  • Escort service to boarding gate.

Pick-up and drop off services from and to Queen Alia International Airport (QAIA) to Abdali Hospital and/or hotel, transport to the appointments during your stay and transport back to the airport can be provided through our International Patient Department. 

 

24/7 airport pick-up and drop off services are a hassle-free option: our staff along with a car and driver will welcome you at the visitors’ lounge and accompany you to your destination. We provide the same services for airport drop off. 

Upon request, a member of our chauffeur-service will be at your disposal for any other local rides.

Clear communication between doctor, patient, and families is essential for building a trustful relationship and making informed decisions. All staff at Abdali Hospital, medical and administrative, speak Arabic and English fluently. If required, we are able to provide you with experienced interpreters that will accompany you during all your appointments and examinations.

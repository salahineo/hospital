About Us

WHO WE ARE

Abdali Hospital is a 200-bed multi-specialty hospital with the mission to provide best practice patient-centred care and promote research and education. Abdali Hospital is founded by the investment group behind the Clemenceau Medical Center in Beirut, affiliated with Johns Hopkins Medicine International. Abdali Hospital benefits from the knowledge and expertise of the Middle East’s finest medical professionals.

Our goal is to improve the health of our community, and internationally, by setting a new standard of excellence in medicine, providing inclusive medical care to prevent, diagnose, manage and treat health problems.

Abdali Hospital promotes medical research and education. At the core of the center’s operations is an uncompromising adherence to integrity and a firm belief that every human being deserves to live their best life. We value humankind; from patients and their families to colleagues and the local community. At Abdali Hospital, exceptional healthcare is about more than cutting edge facilities and advanced technologies: it’s about integrity, quality, compassion, collaboration and accountability at every step of the way.

Our mission is to provide the highest quality, patient-centered healthcare and to promote research, education and a culture of excellence in the MENA region.

We strive to be the region’s leader in clinical excellence, experience and innovation to improve the quality of life of all our patients. 

These are the bases on which our institution stands:

  • Integrity: We adhere to high ethical and professional standards by a commitment to honesty, confidentiality, trust, respect, and transparency.
  • Compassion: We treat our patients, their families, colleagues and our community with sensitivity, empathy, kindness, and respect.
  • Quality: We maintain the highest standards and commit to measuring and improving all aspects of our organization to consistently do the right thing, at the right time, in the right way, for the right person, and with the best possible results.
  • Collaboration: We promote teamwork, value each other’s contributions and share knowledge to benefit the advancement of our mission. We empower patients and their families to participate in decision-making.
  • Accountability: We are committed to providing the best outcomes and experiences of our patients and colleagues. We take feedback, improve and deliver.

PRINCIPLES OF CARE

Abdali Hospital adheres to the following ten principles of care, as a strong part of our Governance

Safety

Patient safety is paramount and without compromise

Evidence Based Medicine

All clinical practice is subject to evidence and regularly reviewed against such

Multi-Disciplinary Care

All clinicians work together in delivering patient-centered care

Leadership

Strong local clinical leadership in place

Comparisons

Benchmarking of quality indicators against local, regional and international data, including peer review

Compliance

Adherence demanded to all local regulatory directives

Professionalism

Following guidance from international professional bodies

Standardization

Standardization of clinical care, where possible

Standards

Regular appraisal of service and staff against JCI and/or other international standards

Patient Experience

Regular patient satisfaction monitoring and improvement

A Unique facility
in the heart of Amman

Strategically located in the vibrant heart of Amman’s newly developed downtown district of Abdali, Abdali Hospital has been designed to reflect the latest international advancements in medical technology, operating systems and design concepts, all with an emphasis on effective, private and comfortable patient care.

Spread across 35 intricately constructed floors, Abdali Hospital has been carefully designed by some of the world’s leading architects, with intuitive, functional considerations for patient inflow and outflow, staff circulation and the segregation of inpatient and outpatient traffic.

INTEGRATED CLINICAL GOVERNANCE, QUALITY PATIENT SAFETY AND OPERATIONAL FRAMEWORK

Abdali Hospital’s clinical excellence is supported through a clear, integrated and robust arrangement of governance committees to lead its clinical governance, quality, and patient safety as well as operations. This framework ensures:

  • Sound Clinical Governance, Quality, and Patient Safety frameworks, structures and processes with clear lines of responsibility and accountability between functions and departments.
  • A flexible mechanism to gather clinical data and information in order to inform and improve clinical risk management and clinical outcomes.
  • An organizational culture of safety and quality improvement, whereby existing practices can be professionally challenged through peer review, and lessons can be learned to facilitate effective quality improvement.

The Integrated clinical governance, quality patient safety, and operational frameworks include the following main governing committees:

  • Clinical Governance, Quality and Patient Safety & Experience Committee (CGQPS)
  • Executive Committee
  • Medical Executive Board
  • Ethical and Professional Conduct Committee

A number of clinical, quality and operational committees underpin the day to day operations to ensure Compliance with Abdali Hospital’s patient safety, quality, patient experience, financial, human resources and regulatory standards.
